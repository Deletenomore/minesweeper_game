Minesweeper Help

Introduction

Minesweeper is a classic logic-based puzzle game where your goal is to clear a grid of hidden mines without detonating them. Use clues provided by the numbers on the grid to identify and flag all the mines.

Game Rules

  • Objective: Clear the grid without clicking on any mines and mark all the mines correctly using flags.
  • Gameplay Basics:
    • The game begins with a grid of covered squares.
    • Clicking a square may reveal:
      • A number (indicating how many mines are adjacent to that square).
      • A blank space (indicating no adjacent mines).
      • A mine (ending the game if clicked).
  • Numbers as Clues: A revealed number shows how many mines are in the surrounding eight squares.
  • Flagging Mines: If you are certain a square contains a mine, place a flag on it to mark it.
  • Winning the Game: Reveal all non-mine squares OR correctly flag all mines to win the game.
  • Losing the Game: Clicking on a mine ends the game immediately.

Controls

  • Left Click: Reveal a square.
  • Right Click: Place or remove a flag.
  • Middle Click (Optional): Reveal adjacent squares if the correct number of flags are placed around a number.

Game Features

  • Difficulty Levels:
    • Beginner: 9x9 grid with 10 mines.
    • Intermediate: 16x16 grid with 40 mines.
    • Expert: 30x16 grid with 99 mines.
  • Custom Mode: Set your own grid size and number of mines for a personalized challenge.
  • Timer: Keep track of how long it takes to solve the puzzle.

Tips and Strategies

  • Start with the corners or edges to quickly uncover large areas.
  • Focus on squares with low numbers firstâ€”they are easier to deduce.
  • Avoid guessing if possible. Use logic to analyze the numbers and deduce mine locations.
  • Use flags strategically to keep track of suspected mines.
  • Look for patterns, such as "1-1-1" or "1-2-1," to identify mine placements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if I make a wrong flag placement?

A: Nothing happens immediately, but it might lead to incorrect assumptions and clicks later in the game.

Q: Can I restart the game?

A: Yes, click the restart button to reset the grid.

Q: Can I pause the game?

A: Some versions of Minesweeper allow pausing. Check if your game includes this feature.
